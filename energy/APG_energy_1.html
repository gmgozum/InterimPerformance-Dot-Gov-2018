Department of Energy  

Launch Critical Materials Recycling Prize

Goal Leader: Steven G. Chalk, Deputy Assistant Secretary for Transportation, Office of Energy Efficiency and Renewable Energy

Goal Statement: The DOE will pursue a focused research program to reduce the supply chain risks posed by the limited availability of critical materials. This program will pursue 1) improvements in domestic production, 2) reuse and recycling, and 3) research into substitutes for critical materials.

  • By the end of Q2 FY 2019, launch a Critical Materials Recycling Prize to spur innovative solutions to solve current challenges associated with collecting, storing, and transporting discarded lithium-ion batteries for eventual recycling; and
  • By the end of Q4 FY 2019, complete Phase I: Concept Incubation and select Phase 2: Prototyping and Partnering winners.
